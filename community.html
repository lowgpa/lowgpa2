Community Rules

All communities follow the same core rules:

  • Strictly Educational
    Discussions must remain strictly related to studies and admissions, especially German university preparation where applicable. This is not a general chat or entertainment space.
  • Respect & Professionalism
    Members are expected to communicate respectfully and professionally. Harassment, aggressive language, or personal attacks are not tolerated.
  • No Spam or Agents
    Spam, promotions, agents, paid services, and unsolicited links are strictly prohibited. This includes private messages sent to members without consent.
  • Zero Tolerance
    Failure to follow these rules may result in removal from the group without prior warning.

A Note Before You Join

Joining a community is optional. If you are unsure which group fits you, it is better to first review the guidance and understand your next step before joining any group.

The goal of these communities is not volume, but useful, honest discussion.

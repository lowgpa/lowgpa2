Serious Groups Access

These groups are restricted to maintain a high-quality, focused environment for students who are actively applying.

Why Restricted?

  • To prevent spam and irrelevant discussions.
  • To ensure every member is committed to the goal.
  • To keep the ratio of serious queries to noise high.

How to Join?

You generally need to demonstrate that you have started your application process (e.g., have shortlisted universities, prepared documents). Access is often granted via the open groups or occasional intake forms.
